How it works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

Tell Eat about yourself once. It handles everything else — forever.

Step 01 🎯

Plan

Tell us your goal, your restrictions, what you're craving this week. Eat builds a full meal plan around you in seconds — powered by Claude AI.

Step 02 🛒

Shop

Your precise grocery list goes straight to your nearest supermarket — Sainsbury's, Tesco, Aldi and more. Order online in a tap. No wandering the aisles.

Step 03 🍽️

Done

Collect or get it delivered. Everything you need, nothing you don't. No waste. No overspending. Just quality food that fits your life.

Features

Every detail.
Thought through.

Eat solves the real problem — the logistics between wanting to eat well and actually doing it.

🧠

AI meal generation

Every plan is generated fresh by Claude, not pulled from a database. Your meals are actually yours.

♻️

Fridge-first planning

Tell Eat what's already in your fridge. It builds your plan around it — less waste, lower grocery spend.

🔥

Craving-aware

Feeling smoky and charred this week? Bright and zesty? Your plan bends around you, not the other way around.

🏪

Supermarket export

One tap sends your precise grocery list to Sainsbury's, Tesco, or Aldi — ready to collect or get delivered.

🥑

Deep dietary support

Halal, keto, gluten-free, carnivore, whole foods — Eat actually understands your restrictions, not just filters for them.

👨‍👩‍👧

Household plans

Planning for two? A family? Eat scales every meal and grocery list to your household size automatically.
