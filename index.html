How it works

Three minutes to
a full week of meals.

No spreadsheet. No paywalls hiding the good stuff. Just a quick conversation, and a plan that feels like it was made for you. Because it was.

i.

Tell us about you

Your goals, your taste, your dislikes, your schedule. Even what's already in your fridge. We listen first, plan second.

ii.

Get a plan that fits

A full week of meals tailored to your goal, your cooking level, and the time you actually have. Swap anything in one tap.

iii.

Shop, cook, enjoy

Auto-built grocery lists. Step-by-step cook mode. Smart leftovers. The boring parts of eating well, finally automated.

Personal · Not generic

A plan that knows the difference between Tuesday and Saturday.

Most meal apps give you the same seven recipes everyone else gets. Eat builds a plan around your real life. Quick lunches on workdays, something slower on weekends, lighter when you said you wanted lighter.

  • Adapts to your goals: fat loss, muscle, just feel better
  • Remembers what you loved and hated
  • Respects your schedule and energy
