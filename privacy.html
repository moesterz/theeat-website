Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

The short version: Eat never sells your data. We collect only what we need to run the service. Your meal preferences stay on your device unless you choose to sync them.

This Privacy Policy describes how Eat collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Eat app and website (theeat.app).

1. What We Collect

Account information: Name, email address, date of birth, and password (securely hashed). If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the information you authorise.

Questionnaire data: Age, weight, height, dietary restrictions, food preferences, budget, and goals — used solely to generate your meal plan.

Usage data: Basic anonymised analytics such as which features you use and crash reports. Never linked to your identity.

Payment information: We do not store payment details. All billing is handled by Apple through the App Store.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone without explicit permission
  • We do not build advertising profiles
  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties — ever

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalised meal plans using AI
  • Remember your preferences between sessions
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, subscription receipts)
  • Improve the service

4. AI Processing

Your meal preferences are sent to Claude (Anthropic) to generate your plan. This is processed under Anthropic's data policies. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). Plan data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced if you are signed in. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

6. Your Rights (UK/EEA)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Contact us at privacy@theeat.app to exercise any of these rights.

7. Children

Eat is not intended for children under 13. Household member data (such as children added by a parent) is managed by the account holder.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and cloud storage
  • Anthropic Claude — AI meal plan generation
  • Spoonacular — food photography
  • Apple App Store — subscription billing
  • Cloudflare — API proxy and security

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Eat after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@theeat.app
