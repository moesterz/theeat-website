Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Eat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Account information — name, email address, and authentication details when you sign up.
  • Questionnaire data — your dietary goals, restrictions, food preferences, and body statistics. This is used solely to generate your meal plan.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, to improve the experience.
  • Location — only if you allow it, to find nearby supermarkets. Never stored on our servers.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data to third parties.
  • We never share your personal information with advertisers.
  • We never store your grocery list data beyond your active session.

How we use your data

Your data is used to personalise your meal plans, generate your grocery list, and improve the Eat experience. Meal plans are generated using Claude AI (Anthropic) — your questionnaire data is sent to their API to produce your plan. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.

Subscriptions

Subscription payments are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

Data retention

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Upon deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@theeat.app
